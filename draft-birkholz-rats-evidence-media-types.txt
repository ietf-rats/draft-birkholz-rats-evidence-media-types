     Media-Type Definitions for TPM 2.0 Generated Evidence Formats
            draft-birkholz-rats-evidence-media-types-latest

Abstract

   Evidence payloads used in Remote Attestation Procedures may require
   an associated media type for their conveyance, for example when used
   in RESTful APIs.  This memo defines media types (and CoAP Content-
   Formats) for evidence generated by TPM 2.0 attesters.
